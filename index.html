Why You Need a Biofouling Management Plan

A Biofouling Management Plan (BFMP) outlines how a vessel minimises the accumulation of marine growth on submerged surfaces. It is increasingly required by flag states and port authorities to reduce the transfer of invasive aquatic species and demonstrate responsible environmental stewardship.

This standalone generator follows IMO Resolution MEPC.207(62) and aligns with Australian and New Zealand biosecurity expectations. Completing the plan helps you stay compliant, reduce fuel costs, and avoid unexpected port delays linked to biofouling inspections.
